## Process & Timeline

**Q: How long does the credentialing process take?**
A: Credentialing typically takes 2–4 weeks once all required documents are submitted. The timeline depends on how quickly you can upload your documents and how promptly licensing boards respond to verification requests.

**Q: What happens after I complete my intake?**
A: Once your intake is complete, your assigned recruiter will contact you within 1–2 business days to discuss available opportunities, compensation, and next steps. You will also receive a credentialing checklist to begin document collection.

**Q: How do I know what documents I need?**
A: You will receive a personalized credentialing checklist by email and SMS after your intake. It lists every required document by category. You can also ask at any time for an update on your checklist status.

**Q: How do I upload my documents?**
A: You will receive a secure, time-limited upload link for each document category. Links expire after 72 hours. If yours has expired, reply to request a new one.

## Placement & Assignments

**Q: What types of assignments are available?**
A: We offer travel, per diem, contract, and permanent placement across hospitals, clinics, long-term care, home health, behavioral health, and ambulatory surgery centers nationwide.

**Q: Can I choose my location?**
A: Yes. During intake we collect your preferred states and markets. Your recruiter will match you with opportunities in those locations based on availability.

**Q: How far in advance do I need to be available?**
A: Most travel contracts require a start date 2–4 weeks out. Per diem and local contract roles can sometimes start within a week. Share your availability date and your recruiter will find the best match.
