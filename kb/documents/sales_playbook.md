# Sales Qualification Playbook
# Approved messaging and qualification logic for SALES-001.
# Do NOT quote pricing, rates, or contract terms. Escalate to sales leader.

## Lead Qualification Framework (BANT)

| Dimension | Question | Qualified Signal |
|---|---|---|
| Budget | Has budget been allocated for staffing? | "Yes, we have budget" or specific FY allocation mentioned |
| Authority | Are you the decision-maker? | Title: CNO, CMO, HR Director, Staffing Manager, VP Operations |
| Need | Specific roles and volume identified? | Named specialty + headcount + location |
| Timeline | Target start date established? | Specific date or "ASAP / urgent" |

## Approved Discovery Questions

1. "What type of facility are you with, and what specialties do you need to fill?"
2. "How many positions are you looking to fill, and in which states or markets?"
3. "What's your target start date — are any of these urgent?"
4. "Who else would be involved in the staffing decision?"
5. "Have you worked with a healthcare staffing agency before?"

## Qualification Scoring

| BANT Score | Tier | Next Action |
|---|---|---|
| 3–4 | Hot 🔥 | Book discovery call immediately |
| 2 | Warm | Send capability overview + book call within 48h |
| 1 | Cold | Nurture sequence + follow-up in 7 days |
| 0 | Unqualified | Tag disqualified + log reason |

## Approved Responses to Pricing Questions

When asked about pricing, rates, or contracts:
> "Great question — our team will prepare a custom proposal based on your specific needs, volume, and location. Let me get a discovery call scheduled so we can walk through all the details."

NEVER:
- Quote bill rates, pay rates, or margins
- Discuss contract terms, guarantees, or SLAs
- Commit to MSP/RPO programs or exclusivity
- Reference competitor pricing

## Escalation Triggers

Route to human sales leader immediately for:
- Any pricing or rate discussion
- Contract term negotiation
- MSP/RPO program interest
- Multi-state or high-volume commitments (50+ positions)
- Existing contract amendments or disputes
